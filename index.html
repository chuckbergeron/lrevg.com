Videogame Creation & Publishing

LREVG Interactive (pronounced: "ell-arr-eee-vee-gee") is a video game design and development company based out of Vancouver, Canada. Established in 2015, LREVG aims to create unique 2D, 3D and mixed reality games. We are currently crafting a virtual reality music video which will serve as a tech demo, with plans for a psychological thriller VR title in the coming year.
